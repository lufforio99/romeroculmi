About This Place:
Before the pandemic, I used to spend most of the week at the dorm. I take the jeep to school. On weekends I go home by the bus, riding through EDSA. The journey takes 2 to 3 hours. The bus drops me off in the middle of the street. I have to run and climb over a gate without getting caught by traffic enforcers.
Fun Fact:
I made a friend on the bus. We don't talk but we always let the other know if our bus is coming and we sit next to each other. He studies at Ateneo. I hope he's doing well.

Do I Recommend:
No. Everyone knows commuting here is awful. But I kinda miss the thrill. Almost getting run over by motorcycles make me feel so alive. I got so good at jumping off of moving vehicles because jeep drivers wouldn't stop for me.
Favorite Memory:
It was nearly 10 pm at MRT. My friend and I were on our way home from a party. The train stopped so we called our friends waiting for us at the dorm. We missed each other even if we've only been apart for half the day.
Worst Memory:
I fell down in the space between the platform and the train once. It was at LRT and my friend saved me. When we boarded the train everyone was talking about me. But they didn't help me!
