Now Playing: the sea breeze and waves against the shore

About This Place:
Our home in Bicol is nearby the sea. Ever since we were kids, we would walk to the ocean and play in the waves and the sand. The beach is closed off so we have to walk along the walls and climb down old stone stairs to reach the shore. Many people swim, fish and surf on this beach.
Fun Fact:
Some parts of this beach is private but we don't know which and we don't care. We visit it for free.

Do I Recommend:
I don't think it will be popular because it has black sand and a lot of sea creatures wash up on shore. It is maintained but there aren't cottages available except for nearby pools.
Favorite Memory:
There was a whale that swam to the shallow part and fishermen and other swimmers pushed it back to the sea. I did not help because they got it under control. I hope the whale got home safely.
Worst Memory:
A sea cucumber clung to my arm and I thought it was gross. I stepped on a starfish chilling on the seafloor. I'm sorry Patrick.
